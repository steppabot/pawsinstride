About Us

Welcome to Paws in Stride, where we believe that every dog deserves a safe, fun, and engaging walk. My name is Greg Saint and I’ve been a dog owner for over 15 years. I’m the proud pet parent of a lovable Shihpoo named Chi, who enjoys nothing more than a good walk and a game of fetch. Having lived in Frisco, Texas for the past 12 years, I’ve grown to love this community and understand the importance of finding reliable care for our furry friends. That’s why I started Paws in Stride. Our goal is to provide exceptional dog walking services that keep both dogs and their owners happy.

Our Services

Safety First: Your pet’s safety is our number one priority. Each walk is GPS tracked, giving you peace of mind and full visibility on your pet’s whereabouts. We make sure every walk is planned with your dog’s well-being in mind, from keeping an eye on surroundings to using the right equipment.

Personalized Care: At Paws in Stride, we understand that every dog is unique. Whether your dog prefers leisurely strolls or high-energy walks, we tailor each outing to meet their individual needs. We also share videos and photos from your dog’s walk, so you can enjoy their adventures and feel connected even when you're apart.

Reliability: We know how important punctuality and reliability are when it comes to caring for your dog. That’s why we ensure our team is always on time, rain or shine. You can count on us to be there when scheduled, delivering consistent and dependable care for your pet.

Connect with Us: We understand that every dog and pet parent has unique needs, and we’re here to listen. Whether you have specific questions about our team, want to learn more about how we handle walks, or would like to discuss your pet's special requirements, we’re just a message or phone call away.

Walking Your Way to a Healthier Dog!

Physical Health Benefits:

  • Improved Cardiovascular Health: Regular walks help maintain your dog’s heart and lung health, reducing the risk of obesity and related issues.
  • Stronger Muscles and Joints: Daily exercise keeps your dog’s muscles and joints strong, reducing the likelihood of arthritis as they age.
  • Better Digestion: Walking helps regulate your dog’s digestive system, preventing constipation and other gastrointestinal problems.

Emotional Health Benefits:

  • Reduced Loneliness: Regular walks provide social interaction and companionship, helping your dog feel loved and less isolated.
  • Lowered Anxiety: Physical activity reduces stress and anxiety, making your dog feel more relaxed and content.
  • Happier Disposition: A well-exercised dog is often a happier dog, showing fewer behavioral problems and more positive interactions.

Mental Health Benefits:

  • Enhanced Mental Stimulation: Exploring new sights and smells during walks keeps your dog’s mind sharp and engaged.
  • Reduced Boredom: Daily walks prevent boredom, which can lead to destructive behaviors like chewing or excessive barking.
  • Better Sleep: Regular exercise helps regulate your dog’s sleep patterns, ensuring they get the rest they need to stay healthy and alert.
